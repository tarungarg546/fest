Robo-Rush Date:-12 & 13 March,2015     Time:-1:00-3:00 PM

Thrust Water Rocketry

Problem Statement:-
Design and construct a water propelled rocket pressurized with air to compete against different constraints in separate rounds.

Game Play:-

Round 1:-
In this round the team whose rocket will remain in air for maximum time i.e. with maximum time of flight will be selected for next round. The design of the rocket and stability in the air would also be the factors of consideration. The participants have to launch their rocket vertically upwards.

Round 2:-
The objective of this round is to make it go as far as possible from the launch point i.e. the maximum horizontal distance.

Rules:-

1-The horizontal distance will be considered from the starting point to the point where any part of the rocket first touches the ground.
2-Multi-stage rockets are also allowed but the combined tank capacity must be minimum 1 litres and maximum 2 liters.
3-Better design would be selected in case of tie.
4-Each team shall be allowed two chances to launch and the better of the two will be considered. (This may be altered later based on the performance of most teams.)
5-Pressure limit for this round will be 75 psi.
6-Participants have to make their own rocket launchers. However air pump shall be provided by organizers.
7-Max no. of members allowed in a team is 3. They can be from same or different colleges.
8-Organizers reserve the rights to change the rules at any point of time as they deem fit. The changes will however be highlighted on the website. So keep checking our website for latest updates.
