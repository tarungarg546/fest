Insurrection

Rules:

1)The competition shall be run in two rounds.
2)You will be given 4 minutes to speak(3+1 for conclusion).
3)The topic will be given one day prior to the event.
4)There will be two questions in rebuttal , from the audience and the judges.
5)The second round is a one on one extemporary debate event for the ones selected in the first round.
6)Contestants will be judged on basis of content, presentation , body language and rebuttal.
7)Paper reading is not recommended.
8)Marks will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.

“NO SWORDS OR BULLETS TO HELP,USE THY WORDS TO INVALIDATE YOUR OPPONENT.”

JAM

Just a Minute is a fun filled, exciting, witty, electrifying event, where grammar, pronunciation and fluency of participants are put to an ultimate test.  The Queen's  language dictates the rules to be followed. Within a speaker time of just one minute, the participants have to speak meaningful, grammatically and phonetically accurate statements. Any flaw in the formation or the presentation of the statement and BANG, the speaker gives away the speaking authority to the participant who pointed out the flaw. The participant with the maximum speaking time takes away the winning baton. An event to test spontaneity, fluency and command over the language, it attracts a horde to people willing to  master the art of accurate speaking.

Rules:

1)Language: English only.
2)Of the group of participants, one starts speaking on a topic given on the spot.
3)A mistake of any kind to upset the rules of British English leads to other participants banging on the table. 
4)The one who bangs first gets to point out the flaw and continue from there on. 
Rule 1: The JAM Master is God.
P.S. In case of any doubts, refer to the rule mentioned above.

Kaavyaotsav English

Awaken the poet, the performer and the admirer in you at one of the most awaited of events. A platform, a distinguished jury and a bunch of self-confessed poets. We provide the much needed push to unfurl your works to the world’s awe.

Rules:

1. There is no restriction on languages(Major Languages- English, Hindi and entries in regional languages like Punjabi, Haryanvi, Awadhi and Bhojpuri would also be welcomed)
2. Every participant can send a maximum of two poems.
3. If entries are sent in two or more than two languages, then a maximum of two poems would be selected.
4. There are no restrictions on the upper limit of a poem but a minimum of ten lines must be there.
5. The number of poems to be selected from a particular language would not be decided beforehand. After receiving all the entries, the organizing panel would be selecting them.
6. During the main event, every participant would get a chance to recite his/her poem on the stage.
7. Every participant would be given a time of five minutes.
8. The poets are not required to explain the meaning of each line as it undermines the very purpose of poetry but are free to use references that enhance the beauty of the poem.

Some of the best entries would be felicitated by the judging panel.
All the prizes would hold equal value and their only motive would be to encourage the poets

काव्योत्सव

एक शाम कवियों की, एक उत्सव काव्य का. जब हवाओं में तैरेंगी कवितायें और शायरी घुली होगी लफ्ज़ों में, तो आइए शामिल हो जाइए कवियों के काव्योत्सव में.

प्रतियोगिता के नियम :

1. भाषा की कोई बाध्यता नहीं होगी ( मुख्य भाषाएँ – हिंदी, इंग्लिश के अलावा क्षेत्रीय भाषाओँ जैसे पंजाबी, हरयाणवी, अवधी, भोजपुरी इत्यादि में भी कविताओं का स्वागत किया जायेगा )
2. हरेक प्रतियोगी हर भाषा में अधिकतम दो कवितायें भेज सकेगा.
3. दो या दो से अधिक भाषाओं में प्रविष्टियां भेजने पर अधिकतम दो भाषाओं की प्रविष्टियाँ चुनी जाएँगी
4. कविता में शब्दों या पंक्तियों की अधिकतम संख्या जैसी कोई बाध्यता नहीं होगी परन्तु न्यूनतम 10 पंक्तियां अनिवार्य रहेंगी.
5. किस भाषा में कितनी कवितायें चुनी जायेंगी, ये पूर्व निर्धारित नहीं होगा. सभी कवितायें प्राप्त हो जाने के बाद आयोजक पैनल कविताओं का चुनाव करेगा.
6. काव्योत्सव के दौरान एक-एक कर के सभी प्रतियोगियों को मंच पर अपनी कवितायें सुनाने का मौका मिलेगा.
7. हरेक प्रतियोगी अधिकतम पांच मिनट का समय ले सकेगा.
8. कवियों को कविता सुनाने के दौरान अपनी कविताओं का मतलब समझाने की अनुमति नहीं होगी ( मतलब समझाने पे कविताओं का महत्व कम हो जाता है और वो सीमित हो जाती हैं ). हालांकि कविता सुनाने से पहले भूमिका के रूप में अपनी कविता का परिचय दे सकेंगे.



निर्णायक मंडल द्वारा चुनी गयी कुछ बेहतरीन कविताओं को सम्मानित किया जायेगा.
पुरस्कारों का वर्गीकरण नहीं होगा. सभी पुरस्कार सामान होंगे और उनका एकमात्र लक्ष्य कवियों को प्रोत्साहित करना रहेगा.
