Solo EventDate:13th March,2015     Venue:Shakuntam Stage

Some people just got to dance. To satisfy this incontrollable urge, YMCA presents Solo and Duo dance competition. All those bitten by the dance bug are cordially invited to relieve themselves. So wear your dancing shoes, let lose your moves and break a jig. Show us what you got, Jhalak dikhlaja....

Time:-Participants are required to report to the Shakuntalm Stage help desk by 11am on 13th March 16.6715.

RULES

1. Only Solo entries allowed.
2. Participants will be given 3+2 minutes to perform.Participants exceeding this time limit would be penalized.
3. All dance forms are welcome.
4. There is restriction on the use of fire or any inflammable object and glass shatter on stage. Negative points may be awarded in such a case.
5. Participants are required to restore the stage to its initial condition after their performance.
6. In case of conflicts, judges descion would be considered final.
7. The Particpants must bring their songs in a pen-drive/CD in mp3 audio format.


EVALUATION CRITERIA:- The performances will be judged by our panel based on skill, choreography, presence of mind, confidence and stage presence
. The result will be declared after all the performances.

Awards :
Each Participant shall be given Participation Certificate by the University
Handsome cash prize for the winning participants.

On the spot registrations are allowed!

Folklore Date: 12th March 2015    Prelims Time: 2:00 PM
Prelims Venue:F8     Finals Venue:Shakuntlam

Folk dance is a form of dance developed by a group of people that reflects the traditional life of the people. FOLKLORE is one of the events under Group Dance category.

Rules:-

Prelims

1. The maximum time allowed for the performance is 4+2 minutes. (Should be a part of team's final performance).
2. Maximum team size: 20 - there must be at least 6 members on stage at any point during the performance.
3. There is restriction on the use of fire or any inflammable object and glass shatter on stage. Negative points may be awarded in such a case.
4. The teams should restore the stage to its previous condition after their performance.
5. The teams must bring pendrives/CDs for the music track(mp3 audio format).
6. Selected teams will go in the final round.


Finals

1. This round is a free style dancing round in which all kinds of dance are allowed.
2. Maximum time allowed for the performance is 8+4 minutes. Teams exceeding this time will be penalized.
3. A minimum of 6 members must be on stage at any time during the performance.
4. There is restriction on the use of fire or any inflammable object and glass shatter on stage. Negative points may be awarded in such a case.
5. The teams should restore the stage to its previous condition after their performance.
6. The teams must bring pendrives/CDs for the music track(mp3 audio format).


EVALUATION CRITERIA:- The performances will be judged by our panel based on skill, choreography, presence of mind, confidence and stage presence
. The result will be declared after all the performances.

Awards :
Each Participant shall be given Participation Certificate by the University
Handsome cash prize for the winning participants.
